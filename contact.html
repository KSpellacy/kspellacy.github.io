Website request checklist

Copy and paste these questions into an email so I can understand your project.

  • Project name: What is the business, program, or personal site name?
  • Goal: What should the website help you do?
  • Pages or sections: Home, About, Services, Portfolio, Contact, etc.
  • Style: What colors, design style, or websites do you like?
  • Content: Do you already have photos, a logo, wording, or branding?
  • Domain: Do you already own a domain name?
  • Timeline: When would you like the website finished?
