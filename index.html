STARS — Community STEM Outreach

What STARS is

STARS (Service-oriented Technology, Aviation, and Robotics for Scholars) is a student-led outreach program I founded to make aviation and technology approachable through hands-on learning. Sessions are built to be safe, engaging, and repeatable, with clear learning goals and strong station flow.

What a session looks like

  • Quick safety brief and expectations
  • Hands-on stations (drones, flight, robotics, cybersecurity)
  • Short debrief connecting concepts to real careers
  • Take-home pathways to keep learning

Modules I’ve built and led

  • Drone fundamentals and safe operating practices
  • Aerodynamics basics: lift, drag, stability, control
  • Flight simulation and aviation workflow
  • Robotics fundamentals and iterative improvement
  • Cybersecurity fundamentals (age-appropriate)
