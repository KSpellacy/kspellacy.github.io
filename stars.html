STARS

Service-oriented Technology, Aviation, and Robotics for Scholars.

STARS is a student-led outreach program designed to make aviation and technology approachable through hands-on learning.

The mission

STARS helps students experience aviation, robotics, drones, and cybersecurity through safe, organized, hands-on stations with clear learning goals.

Session structure

  • Quick safety brief and expectations
  • Hands-on stations for aviation and STEM topics
  • Short debrief connecting concepts to careers
  • Take-home pathways for continued learning

Modules

Modules can be adjusted by age range, available equipment, and event goals.

Drones

Drone fundamentals, safe operating practices, and mission thinking.

Aviation

Lift, drag, stability, control, instruments, and flight simulation basics.

Robotics

Iterative improvement, teamwork, testing, and systems thinking.

Cybersecurity

Age-appropriate security fundamentals and problem-solving concepts.

Leadership

Team roles, communication, safety culture, and event execution.

Career Pathways

How STEM connects to aviation, technology, public service, and future careers.
